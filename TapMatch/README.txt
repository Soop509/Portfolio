This is a static web programme complete with GUI. 
The game allows two players to complete at a tap match where the winner taps faster the his/her opponent
A bar in the center of the screen serves as a preportional representation of how fast both players are tapping.
If Player "A" is tapping faster his/her color will take more space in the bar
A timer just below it the bar counting down from 30 to 0.
If the timer reaches zero before the winner is decided by who's color is greater in size.
After the result the game then has to ber reset by clicking the "RESET" button.
